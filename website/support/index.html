Questions about a loyalty card, Wallet pass, or TapStamp account? We are here to help.

Email hello@tapstamp.co

We usually reply within one business day.

Common help

I lost my digital loyalty card

On the cafe’s tap link, choose Lost your digital wallet? or Replace my card. Check Apple Wallet / Google Wallet first so you don’t add a duplicate. You can also restore by email if you gave one when you joined.

My Wallet pass isn’t updating

Open the pass once after a stamp, or remove and re-add it from the cafe’s tap page using Lost your digital wallet?. Make sure you only keep one copy of the card.

I’m a business owner

For orders, billing, stamps, or the owner app, email hello@tapstamp.co from the address on your account. Include your business name.

Google Wallet / Apple Wallet issues

Email us with your cafe name, phone type (iPhone or Android), and a short description of what happens when you tap Add to Wallet.
