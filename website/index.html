FAQ

When am I charged for software?

14 days after you go live in the owner app — when you link your stamp. The £35 stamp is paid at order.

What counts as a customer on Starter?

A unique person who receives at least one stamp in a calendar month. Same person stamping 5 times = 1 customer.

Do my customers need an app?

No. They tap your stamp and add a card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet.

How do I sign in to the owner app?

Use the email and password you created when ordering. We'll email you when the app is ready to download.
