Questions

Do customers get notifications?

Yes. When you add a stamp or unlock a reward, their phone can alert them — like major wallet loyalty programmes. They open Wallet and your card is already updated.

Is Starter really free after the trial?

Yes. After your 14-day trial at go-live, Starter costs nothing monthly as long as you stay under 50 unique customers per calendar month. Each person who stamps counts once — five visits from the same person still counts as one. The counter resets on the 1st every month.

Do customers need to download an app?

No. They tap your link or stamp and add a pass to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet.

What if I outgrow Starter?

Upgrade to Pro from the owner app — unlimited customers, campaigns, and deeper analytics.

What counts as one customer on Starter?

One person who receives at least one stamp in a calendar month. The same regular counts again in a new month — your 50 slots reset on the 1st. Five visits from one person in the same month still counts as one.
